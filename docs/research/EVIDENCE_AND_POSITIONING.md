# Evidence and Positioning Brief

## Research Discipline

StillMind uses four separate labels: source teaching, established evidence, product interpretation, and validation hypothesis. The source material is not treated as scientific evidence. Numeric consciousness levels, soul/energy mechanisms, population percentages, and guaranteed outcomes are excluded from product claims.

## Evidence-Supported Foundations

- Observer-perspective reflection and self-distancing: [2022 meta-analysis, PubMed 36256910](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36256910/).
- Third-person self-talk and emotion regulation: [Scientific Reports 2017, PubMed 28674404](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/28674404/).
- Body scan meditation: [2022 systematic review and meta-analysis, PubMed 35538557](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35538557/).
- ACT, psychological flexibility, and defusion mechanisms: [2024 review, PubMed 38615492](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38615492/) and [internet ACT review, PubMed 36040783](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36040783/).
- Open monitoring meditation taxonomy: [2008 review, PubMed 18329323](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/18329323/).
- Forgiveness interventions: [systematic review and meta-analysis, PubMed 27009829](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/27009829/).
- Voluntary slow breathing physiology: [2022 systematic review and meta-analysis, PubMed 35623448](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35623448/).
- Planning and implementation intentions: [Annual Review 2025, PubMed 39823206](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39823206/).
- Digital habit-formation intervention patterns: [2024 systematic review, PubMed 38787601](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38787601/).
- Habit formation timing and determinants: [2024 systematic review and meta-analysis, PubMed 39685110](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39685110/).

Evidence does not justify treatment claims, guaranteed relief, or claims that one mechanism works for every user. StillMind measures self-reported immediate usefulness and stopping/worsening, not clinical efficacy.

## Source-to-Product Translation

| Source concept | Product interpretation | Claim boundary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Automatic reaction programs | Notice temporary reaction loops | Do not call users robots or assign a fixed type |
| Reaction flexibility | Pause and choose among reversible actions | Do not claim a consciousness score increase |
| Subjective translation/filtering | Separate observation from interpretation | Do not assert the app knows the event's true meaning |
| Macro observation and detail awareness | Wide gaze, orienting, and factual scene description | Gaze exercise remains experimental |
| Calm handling | Short state regulation before response | Not a treatment for anxiety or anger disorders |
| Self-correction and repeated practice | Weekly review and one next experiment | No moral ranking or punishment streaks |
| Static inner narratives | Inner Cinema and person-shift language | AI output is a lens, not an analysis |
| Release | Optional release/forgiveness method with boundaries | Never require forgiveness or reconciliation |

## Positioning

- Category: an in-the-moment state-shift app for emotional triggers and overthinking.
- Promise: StillMind helps you notice the inner movie, step out of the role, and choose what to do next.
- Wedge: explicit state routing, no diagnosis or identity labels, offline core, optional AI, and a grounded-action ending.
- ICP hypothesis: adults 22-45 who overthink or react strongly after work and relationship triggers, know mindfulness, and dislike long generic sessions.

## Validation Risks

- Users may understand the metaphor but not experience meaningful distance.
- Triggered users may refuse any reading or inward practice.
- Multi-method choice may create friction unless routing is excellent.
- Spiritual-community users and mainstream wellness users may want incompatible language.
- Subscription willingness may be low if use is sporadic.
- AI personalization may feel invasive or generic; preset/offline paths must remain first-class.

## Product Decisions From Research

- Native Expo app, not a WebView wrapper.
- Local-first storage and no account for first value.
- All core methods deterministic and offline.
- Optional server AI only after explicit consent.
- Native haptics, local reminders, offline sessions, data export/delete, and platform-quality accessibility create real iOS utility.
- "Awakening" may remain founder/community context, but public claims use observation, distance, choice, and grounded action.
